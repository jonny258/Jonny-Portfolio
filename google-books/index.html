Project Overview

Google Books API Integration:
Our platform seamlessly integrates with the Google Books API, granting users access to Google's extensive collection of literary works. Users can effortlessly browse through a rich assortment of titles, from timeless classics to contemporary masterpieces, and save any titles that resonate with their interests. With a simple search query, users can uncover a treasure trove of reading material.

Memory Posting:
Our platform acts as a personal digital library, allowing users to curate a collection of books that intrigue them or that they intend to read in the future. The platform provides users with full control over their collections. If a book no longer aligns with a user's interests or if they have completed it, they can effortlessly remove it from their collection with a simple click.

Profile Customization and User Authentication:
The application is designed with a comprehensive user authentication system. It boasts a fully functioning sign-up and login capability, ensuring the security and personalization of each user's experience. This feature allows households or devices with multiple users to maintain separate profiles effortlessly, each with its own unique collection of saved books and preferences.
